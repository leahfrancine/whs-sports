Volleyball Workouts

Day One

Before working out, Warm-Up/Stretch first!

-TODAY’S WORKOUT: BLOCKING DRILLS-

One-Step Blocking Left

Reps: 10
Make a big step with your left foot to the left, closing in with your right, jumping with your arms up in the air and slightly press down your fingertips to block.

One-Step Blocking Right

Reps: 10
Make a big step with your right foot to the right, closing in with your left, jumping with your arms up in the air and slightly press down your fingertips to block.

One-Step Passing Left

Reps: 5

-WARM-UPS/STRETCHES-

Jog

Jog at your own pace! Adjust your speed if you feel exhaust while jogging.

5:00



Lunges

Reps: 10
Position yourself with one leg forward and resting on the knee of the back leg. Be sure that the front ankle is directly under the knee and that the trailing leg is straight out behind you. You may use your hands on the ground to steady the body. Gently lower the hips downward and forwards and hold that position. Repeat on the other side.

Side Lunges

Reps: 10
Start standing with legs slightly wider than shoulder-distance apart and toes pointed forward. Shift your body weight to one leg bending the knee until it reaches a 90-degree angle and the other leg is straight. Glutes are pressing back behind you. Return to center and switch sides.

Cherry Pickers

Reps: 10
Step with one foot forward and using both arms, slowly lift them up from the ground. Alternate with each foot.

Frankensteins

Reps: 10
Take a step and kick your opposite leg straight up. The kicking leg should remain straight and touch the hand of your outstretched arm.

Toe Walk Ins

Reps: 10
Walk on your tippitoes.

Heel Walk Ins

Reps: 10
Walk on the heels of your feet.

Walk Ins Using Side of Feet

Reps: 10
Walk on the side of your feet.
